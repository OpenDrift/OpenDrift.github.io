Open source oil trajectory model based on the OpenDrift framework.

The OpenOil oil drift model is described e.g. in Rohrs, J., Dagestad, K.-F., Asbjornsen, H., Nordam, T., Skancke, J., Jones, C. E., and Brekke, C.: The effect of vertical mixing on the horizontal drift of oil spills, Ocean Sci., 14, 1581-1601, https://doi.org/10.5194/os-14-1581-2018, 2018.

Example usage:

from opendrift.models.openoil3D import OpenOil3D
o = OpenOil3D()
